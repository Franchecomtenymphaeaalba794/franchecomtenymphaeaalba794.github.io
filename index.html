Questions, answered

a few things mamas ask
Is it good for first-time moms?

Yes — every page is written gently, with no assumption of experience. Prompts guide you week by week.

What size is the printed book?

7×10 inches (17.8×25.4 cm), paperback — a comfortable, holdable size with room for photos and writing.

Do I need to write a lot?

Never. A sentence or two is enough. Some weeks you'll just tape an ultrasound and smile.

Is it a good gift?

Beautifully so — for a baby shower, a pregnancy announcement, or the mom-to-be who has everything else.
