## 🛠 Features explained

### Exposure correction
This feature detects dark or bright spots in your photo. It balances the distribution of light so your subject pops out. The tool automatically corrects shadows and highlights to create a natural look.

### Adaptive color adjustment
The software studies the color space of your image. It identifies shifts that look unnatural and corrects them. This is useful for scenes with indoor lighting where walls or fabrics might look discolored.

### Contrast optimization
Contrast defines the depth of your image. Instead of a basic global contrast slider, this tool looks at specific parts of the image to apply changes. It keeps your blacks deep and your whites bright without washing out the texture.

### Noise reduction
Digital photography often creates grain in low-light conditions. This algorithm separates noise from fine detail. You can pull the slider until the grain disappears while your sharp edges remain clear.

### Batch processing
If you have a collection of photos from one photoshoot, you can apply the same settings to all of them at once. Open the Batch tab, add your photos to the list, choose your saved setting profile, and click Run. The software will process your files in the background.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

**Does this software change my original image?**
No. This tool works by utilizing smart objects. You can double-click the effect layer at any time to reopen the plugin window and adjust your settings again. Your original photo information stays safe.

**How do I update the tool?**
Check this repository page regularly. If a new version exists, download the new file and run it. The installer will replace the old files with the new ones automatically.

**Will this slow down my computer?**
The tool runs on your graphics card. It does not pull resources from your processor. Most modern computers will run this smoothly without affecting Photoshop performance.

**What happens if I get an error during install?**
Make sure Photoshop is closed. If the error continues, restart your computer and try again. Ensure that you installed the software using an account with administrator permissions.

**Can I use this on older versions of Photoshop?**
The tool requires specific libraries found only in Photoshop CC 2024 and newer versions. It will not appear in the menu on older software editions.

## 💡 Tips for better results

*   Use high-quality RAW files for the best results. JPEG files have less data, which limits how much the AI can fix.
*   Adjust the noise reduction slider slowly. Too much reduction creates a plastic texture. Stop moving the slider as soon as the grain goes away. 
*   Always preview your images at 100% zoom. This shows you the actual detail level and helps you avoid over-sharpening your photos.
*   Save your favorite combinations of settings as a preset to save time in the future.
